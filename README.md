Workout app
Project Overview
This platform is designed to empower fitness enthusiasts by allowing them to publish, discover, and share their own workouts and splits.

Starting a fitness journey is often the most difficult part, as it can be hard to know exactly where to begin. The goal of this website is to foster a supportive community where users can help each other by sharing what works for them. Whether you are a beginner looking for guidance or an experienced lifter wanting to share your routine, this platform bridges the gap.

Additionally, users who need a personalized starting point can utilize the Recommendation Tab to receive custom, AI-generated workouts.

Key Features
Publish & Share: Easily create and publish your personal workout routines and splits for the community to see.

Discover Routines: Browse and view the workouts and splits created by other users to find inspiration and guidance.

AI Recommendations: Access the dedicated recommendation tab to generate smart, AI-tailored workout plans based on your fitness goals.

Community-Driven: Built on the idea that sharing knowledge helps everyone succeed in their fitness journeys.
